Take a long recording from a practice session or a gig, and Split Recording cuts it into per-song takes: find the boundaries, trim, name each take — automatically where it can. Transcription runs locally, and a lyric match against the [MusicForge](/projects/musicforge) repertoire names a take with its title, when possible. It can also estimate each song's tempo and drift.

Published takes land in a web catalog the band plays from their phones — sorted by date or by song, with a now-playing card and shareable deep links. It replaced SoundCloud.
