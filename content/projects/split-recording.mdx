Every practice produces one long recording. Split Recording cuts it into per-song takes: find the boundaries, trim, name each take — automatically where it can. Transcription runs locally, and a confident lyric match against the [MusicForge](/projects/musicforge) repertoire names a take with its canonical title. It also measures each song's tempo and drift.

Published takes land in a web catalog the band plays from their phones — sorted by date or by song, with a now-playing card and shareable deep links. It replaced the SoundCloud subscription that used to do this job, worse, for $100 a year.
