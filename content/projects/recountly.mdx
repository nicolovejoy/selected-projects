A spoken-word journal. Press record, talk, and the transcript appears as you speak; audio and text save to private storage. Each entry gets a title, tags, and a summary from a small model, and the whole journal is full-text searchable with date filters. Twenty-three older written journal entries are imported alongside the spoken ones.

Single-user by design — it's my journal. Passkey sign-in and a phone-installable app are next.
